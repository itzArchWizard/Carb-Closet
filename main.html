Why "Clothe" Carbs?

Naked Carbs are carbohydrates eaten on their own (like a plain piece of toast or an apple). They digest quickly and turn into glucose, shooting straight into your bloodstream. üöÄ

For women with PCOS, we often have insulin resistance. This means our bodies struggle to bring that sugar down, leading to inflammation, cravings, and hormonal imbalances.

Clothing Carbs means adding protein, fat, or fiber. These act like a mesh, slowing down digestion and flattening that glucose curve. üìâ
